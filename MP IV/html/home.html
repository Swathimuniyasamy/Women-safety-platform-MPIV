PORTALS

The User Portal is designed for women who need safety assistance, mental health support, and emergency help.It includes features like an SOS button, AI chatbot support, live location tracking, and counseling services. Users can also access self-help resources and community support groups for emotional well-being. The Counselor Portal is for mental health professionals who provide counseling and emotional support. It allows counselors to manage user cases, offer chat or video-based therapy, share mental wellness resources, and schedule appointments.The platform ensures confidential and secure communication between users and professionals. The NGO & Volunteer Portal helps organizations and volunteers assist women in need. It provides tools for legal aid, emotional support, and safety assistance. NGOs can connect users with resources, shelters, and legal advisors to help them navigate difficult situations.
